About us

The collective strength of more than 1,000 member organizations growing the $1.3 trillion Japan travel industry.

Our Mission

To increase travel to and within the Japan.
We pursue this mission by establishing travel as essential and responsible, advancing seamless and secure travel, shaping solutions to operational challenges and building a strong business. By engaging with Japan Travel, our members shape a thriving and sustainable travel experience.

Who We Are?

Japan Travel Association is the national, non-profit organization representing all components of the Japan travel industry—a key contributor to Japan's economic success. Our mission is to increase travel to and within the Japan, and in doing so, fuel our nation’s economy and future growth.

What We Do?

As the united voice of the industry, Japan Travel prioritizes high-impact issues that matter most to our collective membership. We not only identify the industry’s biggest opportunities and challenges, but also develop the messages and actions to establish travel as essential, propel the industry forward and create positive economic growth.
With unique capabilities, Japan Travel advocates for the whole of the industry to favorably shape the travel experience. We engage our members with the tools, research and resources needed to influence change at the national, state and local levels.
